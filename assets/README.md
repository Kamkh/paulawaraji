## To replace any image

1. Drop your new file into the right path (use the exact filename above)
2. Refresh the browser — the site picks it up instantly

If a file is missing, the site falls back to SoundCloud cover art automatically.

## Hero photo tips

The hero uses `mix-blend-mode: lighten` — dark areas of the photo dissolve into
the black canvas, so the subject appears to emerge from the dark. For best
results:

- **Black or near-black background** in the photo (the darker, the cleaner the dissolve)
- Portrait orientation (taller than wide), 3:4 aspect ratio works best
- Subject framed in the upper portion of the photo (face high in the frame)
- Studio-lit subject with good highlights
- JPG (smaller file) or PNG, at least 800 px on the short side
