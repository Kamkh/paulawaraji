05 / Profile

Composer at work

Paul Awaraji began his journey in audio and music studying classical music at Lebanon's National Higher Conservatory of Music. While studying cinema at IESAV, his passion for film music and sound truly blossomed, often assisting classmates in scoring their short films.

Across his career he has composed and designed sound for award-winning short films, television, documentary and advertising, working with brands and broadcasters including Kellogg's, UNDP, Coca-Cola, Ola Energy, Optique Vision and STC.

In 2019 he was nominated for a Hollywood Music in Media Award for best commercial music, and won Best Short Film Soundtrack at the Miami International Sci-Fi Film Festival for Above. In 2024 he received the Best Film Score award at the Los Angeles Film Awards for the short film Half Way.
